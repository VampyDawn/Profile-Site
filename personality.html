= Introverted

= Extroverted

Due to the fact I have severe social anxiety and health issues I generally didn't like interacting with strangers, as a result, my social anxiety got worse, and as my health declined my chances of fixing it also faded away. The only people I really interact with in real life now are friends and family. Online I'm a lot more interactive and I think I do enough online to not need to recharge in real life. Though I can go long periods of time where I don't interact with anyone online and only do so irl.

= Observant

= Intuitive

I'm a bit more reliant on what I see in front of me in the here and now, and maybe the near future, however I also enjoy talking about the possibilities of the future, such as the development of technology and potential of stuff like VR. Most of my intuitive side comes from watching fantasy anime and just reading fantasy in general, specifically the isekai genre. So I think I'm close to even on both sides, but I'm more grounded in the present cause I don't expect to live to see a lot of the technology that's in the potential future.

= Feeling

= Thinking

My actions generally seem to lean more on my emotions than my thoughts. I don't think it's to a high degree, but I seem to not really think some of the stuff I do through. An example being my questions, I sometimes ask random questions out of nowhere, or change the subject because I thought of something, and in that moments I ask it without really thinking about it. Not entirely sure why I do it but I do.

= Prospecting

= Judging

I'm pretty much even on both sides for this. I don't really plan ahead and I just go with the flow, but the thing is I'm not really good at it, I'm neither good at planning nor dealing with situations in the moment. I tend to be impulsive in my decisions, however I do think about the impulsiveness of my decisions. And a lot of the time, talk myself out of doing something.
